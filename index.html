About Me

Data is a story.
I help tell it.

I'm Turki Altayeb, a Data Science student with a strong focus on data analysis. I specialize in transforming complex datasets into clear, actionable insights that drive real business decisions.

I gained hands-on experience through a 6-month cooperative training at the Jeddah Municipality's Data Office, where I delivered city-scale geospatial analysis and contributed to Vision 2030 planning projects.

With hands-on experience in Power BI, Python, SQL, and Excel, I design end-to-end analytics solutions — from raw data cleaning all the way to polished interactive dashboards. I enjoy working on projects where data can genuinely change how people think and operate.

When I'm not analyzing data, I'm always learning — exploring new tools, techniques, and ways to make data more accessible and impactful.
