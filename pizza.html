Key Insights
  • 01 Top-selling pizza categories drive over 60% of total revenue, revealing a concentrated product mix opportunity.
  • 02 Peak order times cluster around lunch and dinner hours, enabling better staffing and inventory planning.
  • 03 Seasonal trends show consistent sales dips mid-week, suggesting targeted promotions could lift revenue.
  • 04 Large size pizzas generate the highest average order value, pointing to upselling opportunities.
